A twitch chatbot that sends you microbe-themed bingo cards upon request!

How to invite the bot into your chat

  1. Go to twitch.tv/microbebingo and type !bingoenter.
  2. Done! Now chatters on your channel can type !getcard to get a random bingo card!

How to remove the bot from your chat

  1. Type !bingoleave in your chat.
  2. Done! The bot will not send messages in your chat anymore.

How to use the chatgpt integration

  1. Type !theheck.
  2. Wait a few seconds..
  3. Done! The bot should reply in chat with a chatgpt to description of the creature on the screen!
